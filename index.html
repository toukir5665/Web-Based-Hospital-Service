about our facility

learn and explore our facility

ambulance services

Our hospital provides 24/7 ambulance service equipped with modern life-support systems and trained medical staff to ensure quick and safe transportation of patients during emergencies. With a dedicated fleet of ambulances, we guarantee timely response and professional care from the moment help is needed until arrival at our hospital.

emergency rooms

Our Emergency Room operates 24/7 with a dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and support staff ready to handle critical and life-threatening conditions. Equipped with advanced medical technology and rapid response systems, we ensure immediate attention, accurate diagnosis, and timely treatment for every patient who walks through our doors.

free check-ups

We offer free health check-up programs to promote early diagnosis and encourage preventive care in our community. Our skilled medical team provides basic screenings, consultations, and guidance to help you maintain a healthier lifestyle at no cost.
